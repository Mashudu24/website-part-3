About Us

Brief history

Petal whisper is a small black owned business that was founded in 2024 by Mashudu Mudau.
Petal whisper ia an online based business that sources it's flowers from flower markets.
Petal whisper sells flowers for weddings,parties,funerals and for gifting a special somebody.

Our Vision

Petal whisper's vision is to become South africa's most reliable flower business and one day have a physical store
that will have multiple branches across South africa and africa.Our vision is to be a florist that brings communities together and create stronger bonds.
Create a world where FLOWERS bring a fresh meaning to life and whisper timeless designs into the florist world

Our mission

  • QUALITY: Our mission at Petal whisper is to deliver high quality flowers.
  • AFFORDABILITY: Ensure that our products are affordable without compromising the quality and elegance.
  • DELIVERY: Our mission is to provide quick delivery to ensure that your beautiful flowers arrive in perfect condition.
  • SUSTAINABLE: Promote sustainabilty by not over sourcing flowers and making sure the enviroment is not downgraded.
